First tome of my series on vampires. This novel was written for a contest of novice writers with a young adult audience. It is a bit-lit novel, meaning most of the characters are vampires.
Like, I'm guessing, every adult who was a teenager in the 90s, I was really influenced by Anne Rice's novels and Joss Whedon's series Buffy the vampire slayer. The walls of my bedroom were covered with David Boreanaz and James Marsters posters. On my door hung proudly an Interview with a vampire poster.
As for the new generation of bit-lit novels, I have to admit that Twilight definitely bores me. But I was pleasantly surprised by Lindqvist work: Let me in. It has a particularly dark point of view.
